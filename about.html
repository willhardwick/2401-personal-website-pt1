Who am I?

BORING

Hi, I'm Will Hardwick. I'm interested in a wide range of communication arts, media, and technology and how these fields all intersect to create innovative and exciting products, engaging software and web applications, and cohesive, refreshing brand identities for businesses and professionals alike. I myself have skillsets and work experience relevant to these fields, such as photography for both e-commerce and promotional use, social media management, creative direction, and graphic design. Presently, I am primarily focused on building upon my modest amount of skill and experience in web design and software development in hopes to gain employment in a related role.

Other than that, recently I've been into:

Photography

I'm always taking photos wherever I go. I used to enjoy doing staged, conceptual shoots, but recently I have been enjoying taking more candid and spontaneous snapshots of the people in my life and the places we end up. Ultimately, I enjoy lots of different genres of photography and I also have a decent photo-book and magazine collection.
